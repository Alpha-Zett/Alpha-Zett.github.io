Minimum Viable Feature 2
COVID-19 Cases Presentation

Description

A lot of people care about COVID-19 situations in other states of the country and/or other countries. Whether it's their personal interest, or because of their friends and families, this feature can present them with both domestic and international data of COVID-19. The main attributes we plan to present are total number of cases, total number of deaths, total number of recoveries, daily new cases, daily new deaths and infection by population.
