Position Justification
Website Developer Website Developers are responsible for designing, developing and maintaining websites. This role is essential for the future development of our website, as their skills and experience can help: create a more user friendly website design, implement changes proposed by the UX Designer and customers, and regularly update the website with accordance to what is happening.
AI Specialist AI Specialists are experts who have strong knowledge of artificial intelligence technology and know how to implement them. This role is vital for the future development of our product because their skills and experience can aspects of our website automated. More specifically: they can help create an RSS Feed which outputs articles from a wide-range of sources, create a more interactive chatbot, and automate the interactive map so it continuously updates the case numbers daily.
Data Analyst Data analysts are people who collect, process and analyze statistical data. Their skills can help businesses understand how to make better decisions. Their skills and experience is important as it can help the developers identify things that are working well, things that are not working well and opportunities for improvement. These analyses can help the website appeal to a wide range of users and deliver more value to them.
Database Manager The role of a Database Manager is to create and maintain databases, or upgrade the existing database. Their skills and experience is important because it can help enhance the current database being used for the Interactive map and expand it when data structures become more complex.
