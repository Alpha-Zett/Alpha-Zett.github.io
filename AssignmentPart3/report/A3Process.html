Ramon This project has taught me many things about group projects - how to properly plan projects and how the principles of Agile Ways of Working are applied to projects.

Planning Projects
This project taught me how to define objectives, responsibilities and milestones during the early stages of the project. These are really important as establishing these correctly can help the team work efficiently and keep everyone focused on the main goal.

Agile Ways of Working
Throughout this project, I focused on encouraging open communication, simplicity and using working software as the primary measure of progress. This taught me how communication can help improve the efficiency and team dynamic and how emphasizing on simplicity makes the complicated aspects of the project easier to understand. I also learnt how using working software as the primary measure of progress helps the team focus on developing tangible deliverables.

Adapting to Change
This project also taught me how things can change, and the importance of embracing these changes. I learnt how these changes can be an opportunity for everyone to learn something new, to create products in different ways we did not know was possible or identify other ways we can deliver value to users.
Feedback
I used the feedback from my peers and teachers to improve on my individual parts. The feedback I received throughout this project often were: expanding on certain areas, making parts clearer, using the correct notations and applying the right concepts. I also used this feedback as a learning experience for future projects.

Communication
For the majority of the project, our team used Microsoft Teams and GitHub as our platforms for communications. It worked out for the most part - we hosted regular meetings, we discussed questions and concerns we had and occasionally communicated each other’s progress. However, it was difficult to keep track of everyone’s progress with Microsoft Teams alone. Our team started using Trello more frequently in the later stages - which made it much easier to keep track of the tasks that need to be done, that are in progress and that have been accomplished.

Most important aspect of carrying out a project like this
Like most projects, I strongly believe that good teamwork is the most important aspect of carrying out a project like this. As this project involves creating multiple features to deliver value to users, teams must be able to work together to ensure that the team is achieving milestones to the expected standard whilst meeting the deadlines.

My advice
If groups were to embark on a similar project to this, I would highly recommend them to put more emphasis on planning. Making sure that you have a clear picture about what the end product and its features are going to look like, and the key milestones that need to be achieved. I would also advise the team to communicate with each other frequently, as the features rely on each other to make the whole product work well.
