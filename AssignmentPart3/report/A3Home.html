Project Background

Motivation

The idea of this project is driven by the negative impact of COVID-19 on Victorians and people around the world today. The pandemic has caused many confusions and overwhelmed people with many changes and restrictions that took place. Thus, we want to create a platform where we provide Victorians with reliable and detailed information about COVID-19. Doing so will allow us to show Victorians how they can keep their loved ones and themselves safe and stay informed about what is happening around them.
