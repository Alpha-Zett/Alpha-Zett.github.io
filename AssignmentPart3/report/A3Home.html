Project Background

Motivation

The idea of this website is driven by the negative impact of COVID-19 on Victorians today. The pandemic has caused many Victorians to feel confused and overwhelmed with the many changes and events that took place. Here, we want to create a platform where we provide Victorians with the right information, at the right time about COVID-19. Doing so will allow us to show Victorians how they can keep their loved ones and themselves safe and stay informed about what is happening around them.

Aim and Goals

Aim

Our group aims to help Victorians stay safe during the pandemic. We intend to do so by providing real-time data about active COVID-19 cases in Victoria, showcasing risk levels of people around them and providing updates about restrictions and rules. This can help with contact tracing and provide opportunities for citizens and the Government to take proactive and reactive measures, which can minimize the virus spread and help citizens stay positive and healthy during a difficult and confusing time.

Goals

  1. Keeping people informed about COVID-19 in Victoria. We intend to achieve this through the Chatbot and RSS Feeds, which will be used to deliver the latest information about the current rules and regulations, and daily news about COVID-19. We believe that this will help Victorians as they can use this information to take proactive measures to keep their families and friends safe, and also prepare for events that may occur.
  2. Create an easily accessible platform for most data relating to COVID-19 in Victoria. We intend to achieve this by creating a website hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS), as we want our website to be accessible to as many people as possible. We also aim to create our website as user friendly as possible, so users of all ages and backgrounds can easily read and understand the information presented to them.
  3. Keeping people informed about COVID-19 within their suburbs. As COVID-19 affects suburbs across Victoria differently, we want to make sure that Victorians know what is happening around in their area. Providing this information can help Victorians take more precaution areas close to them and help them find their nearest testing facility.

Scope

Our project objective is to create a functional website that provides real-time information about COVID-19 in Victoria, and updates about regulations and restrictions relating to COVID-19. The deliverables are the minimum viable features: Interactive Map, Contact Tracing, Chatbot, COVID-19 Trend and RSS Feed; and the extended features: Regional RSS Feeds, COVID Cases presentations and testing facility locations.

Milestones

  • Week 6-9: Complete feature and website designs
  • Week 10: Complete feature prototypes
  • Week 11: conduct testing and implement necessary changes
  • Week 12: Finalize product

Assumptions and Constraints

Constraints Assumptions
The website may become redundant when COVID-19 dies down. Majority of Victorians have access to internet and functional devices. Hence, we are trying to cater for all suburbs.
The information provided about COVID-19 is limited – therefore we cannot provide detailed information. COVID-19 will be around for six months since the start of this project. Hence, we thought creating this website would be a good service.
Despite getting information from official websites such as DHHS Victoria, the information may not be fully accurate as some people refuse to get tested or other factors. We are assuming that conveying the relevant information will encourage citizens to abide by the rules and regulations.
We have a limited time frame. Therefore, certain features may not be developed properly
Majority of the team members have limited experience with programming and website programming.
